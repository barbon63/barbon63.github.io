It may sound obvious but serving delicious food is one of the key elements of a successful restaurant. You can have a fantastic location, friendly servers and good marketing but customers won't give you
a second look if your food is not up to par. One of the key characteristics of a successful restaurant is a positive customer experience.
The staff who interact with your guests should be courteous and maintain a
positive attitude. Then comes menu pricing. Customers will flock to the competition if your food is too expensive for the location and the ingredients you're offering.
At Urban Bistro we tick all the boxes!!

ABOUT

Urban Bistro is the perfect place to dine in Dublin. This restaurant is in the heart
of the city and upholds the history of the area by maintaining the constant pursuit of
perfection and takes its inspiration from the veritable taste of the Mediterranean.
We create a restaurant experience that you won't be able to stay away from!

The culinary approach is adventurous and thought provoking with classic dishes reinterpreted without
losing their authenticity. Highlighting their trademark of limitless imagination, true passion for
local food, our Chefs pay tribute to both the Irish produce and the traditions of Mediterranean Cuisine.
